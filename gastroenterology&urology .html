Gastroenterology & Urology Services

Advanced care for digestive and urinary health—delivered with precision, privacy, and compassion.

Specialized Care for Two Vital Systems

Our Gastroenterology and Urology departments offer expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of digestive and urinary conditions. From abdominal pain and reflux to kidney stones and prostate issues, we take a thorough, empathetic approach to every case.

With access to advanced diagnostics and minimally invasive procedures, our goal is to restore your health—and your confidence—as quickly and comfortably as possible.

Gastroenterology

Management of acid reflux, ulcers, IBS, liver conditions, digestive inflammation, and endoscopy services.

Urology

Diagnosis and treatment for kidney stones, prostate disorders, UTIs, and urinary incontinence.

Advanced Diagnostics

On-site ultrasound, cystoscopy, colonoscopy, lab work, and digital imaging for accurate, same-day diagnosis.

Personalized, Discreet, and Evidence-Based

We understand that gastrointestinal and urological issues can be sensitive. Our specialists approach every patient with the utmost privacy, dignity, and evidence-based care planning.

  • Men’s and women’s urological care, including prostate and bladder health
  • Diet and lifestyle counseling for digestive wellness
  • Minimally invasive interventions and day procedures available
