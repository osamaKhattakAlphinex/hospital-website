Pharmacy Open 24/7

Round-the-clock access to trusted medication and expert guidance.

Your Health, Our Commitment — Anytime

Our in-house pharmacy operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring that patients and their families always have access to essential medications—no matter the time. Whether it’s a midnight emergency, a post-surgery prescription, or chronic medication refill, we’re here.

With licensed pharmacists on duty at all times, we ensure each patient receives the right dose, the right drug, and the right information.

Essential Medications

Always stocked with painkillers, antibiotics, and post-op prescriptions.

24/7 Pharmacists

Get expert advice on dosage, usage, and side effects anytime.

Instant Prescriptions

Prescriptions filled immediately after your consultation or discharge.

We’re More Than a Pharmacy—We’re Part of Your Care Team

Our pharmacists play an active role in your treatment journey. They work hand-in-hand with physicians, nurses, and care coordinators to ensure your medication therapy is accurate, safe, and effective.

  • Immediate availability of post-emergency or post-surgical medication
  • Review of allergies and interactions before dispensing
  • Guidance on usage, timing, storage, and food interactions

Here’s What to Expect

Step 1

Doctor Prescribes

Your doctor sends an e-prescription directly to the pharmacy.

Step 2

Pharmacist Prepares

Our team prepares your medication quickly and safely.

Step 3

Patient Counselling

You receive detailed instructions on how and when to take your medication.

Step 4

Medication Delivered

Patients admitted or discharged receive medication directly to their bed or home.
