Orthopedic & Laparoscopic Surgery

Precision. Recovery. Confidence. Surgical excellence tailored to your comfort and mobility.

Expert Surgical Care with Minimally Invasive Solutions

Whether you’re dealing with a painful joint, limited mobility, or a surgical condition requiring minimal disruption, our Orthopedic and Laparoscopic Surgery unit provides advanced, patient-friendly solutions.

Our board-certified surgeons use precision techniques, modern equipment, and decades of experience to ensure optimal outcomes with faster recovery and less discomfort.

Orthopedic Surgery

Joint replacements, fractures, sports injuries, and spinal conditions treated with precision and care.

Laparoscopic Procedures

Gallbladder, hernia, appendix, and abdominal surgeries with minimal scarring and faster recovery.

Rehabilitation Focus

Post-surgery rehab and follow-up care to ensure you regain full strength and mobility.

Smaller Incisions, Stronger Results

Our laparoscopic approach uses high-definition imaging and specialized instruments to perform surgery with minimal tissue disruption. This means reduced pain, shorter hospital stays, and quicker return to your normal life.

  • Less scarring and faster healing
  • Reduced risk of complications
  • Shorter downtime with same-day or next-day discharge
