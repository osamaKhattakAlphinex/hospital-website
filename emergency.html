Emergency Care, 24 Hours a Day

Rapid response. Trusted care. Available every moment you need it.

When Seconds Matter, We’re Here

Medical emergencies can happen anytime—day or night. At our state-of-the-art Emergency Department, we are always ready to deliver fast, life-saving treatment. From severe injuries and strokes to heart attacks and trauma, our skilled team is trained to respond with speed and precision.

As a trusted emergency facility in the region, we ensure quick triage, expert evaluation, and immediate stabilization. Our compassionate professionals are trained to act decisively while keeping your comfort and safety in focus.

Expert Emergency Physicians

Our board-certified ER doctors and trauma specialists are trained to handle every critical situation, no matter how complex.

Advanced Life Support Systems

We’re equipped with modern ICU-grade monitors, ventilators, defibrillators, and rapid diagnostic tools to deliver immediate care.

24/7 Ambulance Services

Our on-call ambulance fleet ensures fast transfers with pre-hospital care so treatment begins before you even arrive.

Equipped for Every Possibility

Our emergency department is powered by modern medical technologies that aid in fast, accurate diagnosis and streamlined treatment planning. In-house lab testing, portable X-ray, ultrasound, and CT scans allow our clinicians to act without delay.

  • Dedicated trauma and resuscitation rooms
  • Negative-pressure isolation bays for infectious cases
  • Direct link to ICU, operating theater, and specialists

What Happens When You Arrive?

Step 1

Arrival & Triage

Our triage nurse assesses your condition and assigns priority based on urgency.

Step 2

Initial Treatment

You’ll receive immediate medical attention and stabilization by our ER doctors.

Step 3

Diagnosis

We’ll conduct necessary tests to determine the cause and severity of your condition.

Step 4

Further Action

Depending on your condition, you may be admitted, referred to a specialist, or discharged with guidance.

Need Immediate Help?

If you’re facing a medical emergency, don’t wait. Our doors are open 24/7 to protect and serve your health.
